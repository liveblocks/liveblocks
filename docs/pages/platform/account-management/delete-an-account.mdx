To delete your account and all your data, you can do so directly from your
Liveblocks dashboard. Simply go to the **Settings** section and follow the
instructions to delete your account.

</Figure>

## Important considerations

Please note that this action is **irreversible** and all your data will be
permanently removed. This includes:

- All projects and their associated data
- Room history and collaborative content
- Team member access and permissions
- Billing history and subscription information

## Getting help

If you need further assistance with account deletion or have questions about
what data will be removed, contact us at [support@liveblocks.io](mailto:support@liveblocks.io).
