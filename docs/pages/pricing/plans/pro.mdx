The Pro plan is designed for developers shipping collaborative experiences in
production. It includes everything in Free, but enables you to remove the
"Powered by Liveblocks" badge, and includes unlimited monthly active rooms. You
can also buy the Unlimited Version History add-on.

## Billing cycle

The Pro plan follows a monthly billing cycle that starts on the 1st of each
month and ends on the last day of the month. Monthly credits renew at the
beginning of each billing cycle and are applied to metered usage. Any usage not
covered by credits appears on your invoice.
