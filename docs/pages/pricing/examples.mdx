These examples estimate monthly bills for four common products. Each page walks
through a scenario, the meters that apply, and how
[plan credits](/docs/pricing/overview) cover usage.

Unit prices are on the [Pro](/docs/pricing/plans/pro#Metered-usage) and
[Team](/docs/pricing/plans/team#Metered-usage) plan pages. Track your own usage
on the [dashboard usage page](/dashboard/usage).

## How billing works

Every paid plan includes a monthly credit allowance. Credits are applied to
metered usage first. You only pay overage when usage exceeds your included
credits.

See [Billing](/docs/pricing/billing) for how plan base, credits, and PAYG
overage add up on your invoice. Billing annually saves 2 months on the plan
price; overage rates are the same either way.
